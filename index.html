What We Can Do for You

Rental of equipment

Hobbies can be expensive. That shouldn't be a reason to stop doing what you like best. Hobbycentral takes the financial burden off of practicing. We offer every type of equipment that you'd like, from pratice rooms, to guitar picks. So you can do you. Always.

Easy to use services

The rooms in our rental programme each have a schedule. Anyone can see, anyone can use. Navigate to the 'Schedule' page and find out when is the most fitting time for you. Your own things in your own time. How cool's that?

Team of experts

Practicing can be hard, especially with equipment that isn't yours. Our team of experts in any hobby available will gladly guide you through. Just as any of us. More info below!
